Measure Converter


• Usage of this Temperature Converter:

To use the temperature converter you just need to write the source temperature to be converted and then press 'Enter' (carriage return) and the converted temperature'll be displayed.


• Types of conversions with examples:

1. From Celsius to Farenheit or Kelvin (32C to F, 42C to K)
2. From Farenheit to Celsius or Kelvin (32F to C, 42F to K)
3. From Kelvin to Celsius or Farenheit (32K to C, 42K to F)
4. From Meters to Inches (32M to I)
5. From Inches to Meters (32I to M)
